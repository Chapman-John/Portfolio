Books worth reading

Books I've recently read and enjoyed:

The Beginning of Infinity

By David Deutsch

He brilliantly describes how explanatory knowledge has been the best way for us to make progress. Key points: All knowledge is conjectural and needs to be open to criticism. Problems are inevitable, yet all problems are soluble.

Straw Dogs

By John Gray

Read the first five pages, and it will send any Westerner’s worldview for a spin. In the book, he reminds us not to forget human behavior when it comes to progress, especially progress outside of science.

Guns, Germs, and Steel

By Jared Diamond

If you want to understand what geographical advantages helped the West become prime movers over the last five hundred years, this book presents a hard-to-disprove explanation of history through geography.
